Our Story

Founded in 1978 by John and Mary Henderson, Starlight Cinema began as a single-screen theater with a dream to bring the magic of movies to our community. What started as a humble storefront with 100 seats has grown into the beloved local landmark you know today.

For over four decades, our family has operated this theater with one simple philosophy: movies are best experienced together. While multiplexes came and went, we remained committed to providing a personal touch - greeting every patron by name, popping every batch of popcorn fresh, and carefully selecting films that bring our community together.
